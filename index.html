Graduate Data Science Intern - Nike, Inc.

Location: Beaverton, OR

Duration: Summer '23

Team: Merchant Financial Planning Team

I leveraged a diverse range of advanced machine learning models, including LSTM, Vector Auto Regression, Dynamic Linear Model (DLT), BlockRNN, Exponential Smoothing, ARIMA, Holt-Winters, and Facebook Prophet, to conduct multivariate time series forecasting of Nike’s Retail Sales Value in the NA Men's Footwear 3PW segment. By optimizing the models through techniques like Optuna & GridSearch, I achieved a 36% improvement in backtest accuracy, alongside a 6% WMAPE value, significantly enhancing the precision of our predictions and contributing to better decision-making in sales strategy.
